ABOUT

Sameen Integration Technology Consulting is passionate about helping business partners meet their goals and objectives in the most efficient and effective manner with a sense of urgency, commitment and accountability.

Wamiz Sameen, founder and managing director of Sameen Integration Technology Consulting, is an accomplished executive leader, respected as a strong executor and team builder. He leads by example, has a “Can-do” attitude and has delivered several complex and visible information technology initiatives for multiple Fortune 500 companies. During his 20 years of professional experience, Wamiz has built high performing global integration management offices and achieved proven results.

- Program executive for Meta Reality Labs program leading Metaverse product life cycle management, global trade compliance, dangerous goods and master data management initiatives.

- Program executive for a $95M general insurance program for a Fortune 100 insurance company. Overseeing program management and delivery office responsibilities.

- Structured a M&A technology integration and digital transformation effort as head of PMO. Includes leading IT Integration Management Office, strategy and execution for a publicly traded global medical device company that encompasses establishing a virtual organization from the ground up with a cloud based technology footprint. Helped acquire software development house which is a pioneer in Artificial Intelligence and Virtual Reality specializing in digital spine technology (HoloSurgical). Established IT operations that includes onboarding Managed Support Services and Managed Security Support Services. Lead an organization effort to rollout a comprehensive ERP system to support business operations (Global Sales, Marketing, Distribution, Operations, Finance, 3PL integration etc.). This includes ERP selection, execution and deployment that will meet medical device company FDA regulations, master data management and quality compliance.
